Intranet

An intranet is a private network accessible only to an organization's members, employees, or others with authorization. It is built using internet technologies but is confined within a private environment.

Intranets serve as centralized platforms for internal communication, document sharing, collaboration, and business applications. They are protected by firewalls and access controls to ensure security.

Intranet Network Architecture

Key Intranet Features:

  • Internal Communications: Company announcements, newsletters, and messaging systems
  • Document Management: Centralized storage and sharing of internal documents
  • Collaboration Tools: Team workspaces, project management, and calendars
  • HR Portals: Employee self-service for leave requests, benefits, and policies
  • Application Hosting: Internal business applications and dashboards
